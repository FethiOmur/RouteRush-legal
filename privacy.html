Privacy Policy

Last updated 14 June 2026

RouteRush ("RouteRush", "we", "us") is a fitness app that records your runs and other activities, maps your routes, and provides an optional AI running coach and social features. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. By using RouteRush you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information — when you sign up we store your email address (or Apple sign-in identifier), display name, and an optional profile photo, to create and secure your account.
  • Location data — to record a run we collect precise GPS location while a workout is active, including the route path, distance, pace, and elevation. We do not track your location in the background when you are not recording an activity.
  • Health & fitness data — with your permission we read workout and fitness metrics from Apple HealthKit (such as workouts, distance, active energy, and heart rate) to display and summarise your activity, and we may write completed workouts back to HealthKit.
  • Activity content — runs you save, titles, notes, achievements, and anything you choose to share with clubs or other users.
  • AI coach conversations — if you use the AI coach, the messages you send are processed to generate a response.
  • Usage & device data — basic app analytics and diagnostics (feature usage, crashes, device model, OS version) to keep the app working and improve it.
  • Subscriptions — purchases are handled by Apple. We receive your subscription status from Apple but never see your card or payment details.

How we use your information

  • To record, map, and summarise your activities.
  • To provide social features (clubs, leaderboards, following) you opt into.
  • To generate AI coaching responses when you use that feature.
  • To operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the app.
  • To manage your subscription and entitlements.

Apple HealthKit

Data obtained from HealthKit is used solely to provide in-app features (showing and summarising your activity). We do not use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing, and we do not sell it or share it with third parties for those purposes. You can revoke Health access at any time in iOS Settings › Privacy & Security › Health.

How information is shared

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers that help us run the app, and only as needed:

  • Supabase — hosts our database and authentication (your account, activities, and social data).
  • Apple — sign-in, HealthKit, and in-app subscription processing.
  • Mapbox — renders maps and route overlays.
  • AI provider — when you use the AI coach, your messages are sent to a third-party large-language-model provider to produce a reply.
  • Analytics/diagnostics — aggregated usage and crash data to improve the app.

We may also disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights and users.

Data retention

We keep your account and activity data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your associated personal data, except where we must retain limited records to comply with legal obligations.

Your rights & choices

  • Access and update your profile in the app.
  • Delete your account from the app, which removes your associated data.
  • Control Location and Health permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or erase your data — contact us to exercise them.

Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, authenticated access) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

Children's privacy

RouteRush is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will remove it.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above and, where appropriate, an in-app notice.
